LocalNOAARadio will stream the NOAA weather radio station closest to your current location.  
When you first launch the app it will detect your location and start the broadcast immediately.  
You can enter a new location (like "Monterey California" and it will stream the closest NOAA weather station to that location.

LocalNOAARadio works great with CarPlay.  It continues to stream when the app is in background or your device is in standby.

The quality of these streams is highly variable!  
ometimes they sound great, other times not so much, other times the broadcast source is not working at all and we can't know when they will be back up.   
LocalNOAARadio will automatically switch to the next closest stream if the connection fails.  If it sounds terrible you can tap Next to get the next closest stream.
